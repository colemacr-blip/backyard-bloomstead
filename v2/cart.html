Flower Care

Caring for Your Blooms

A few simple steps will keep your fresh arrangements looking their best for as long as possible.

How long will my fresh arrangement last?

With proper care, most of our fresh-cut arrangements will last 5–10 days. To maximize longevity: trim stems at a diagonal before placing in water, use a clean vase with fresh cold water, and keep your arrangement out of direct sunlight and away from heat sources. Change the water every 2–3 days and re-trim stems with each change for the best results.

How do I care for a Posie Bag?

The Posie Bag is designed as a gift-ready arrangement, so it travels well — but once you’re home, remove the stems from the bag and place them in water as soon as possible. Trim the ends at a 45° angle before placing in a clean vase. The kraft bag can be reused as a planter liner, gift wrap, or keepsake. Each Posie Bag includes a printed care card with detailed instructions.
