Our Story

More than flowers.

We grow cut flowers, create floral art, and build meaningful gifts — all from a real backyard garden. Every product in the Posie line is inspired by the memory of a small rescue cat who loved to nap among the zinnias and dahlias, as if the blooms were her sanctuary.

Her Story

Meet Posie

Posie was a small tortoiseshell rescue cat who wandered in from under the cottage and quietly claimed the garden as her own. She had a particular fondness for napping among the zinnias and dahlias — curling up in the blooms as if they were made just for her.

Her memory lives on in every arrangement we grow and every piece of art we create. The Posie line is Gracen’s way of honoring a fleeting life that left a lasting imprint.
