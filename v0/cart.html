Flower Care

How long will my arrangement last?

Our arrangements typically last 5 to 7 days, depending on the flower varieties and the care they receive. Some blooms — like zinnias, marigolds, and strawflower — are naturally long-lasting, while others are more delicate. Keep them in cool indoor temperatures, change the water daily, and give the stems a fresh trim every couple of days.

How do I care for flowers in a Posie Bag?

The flowers in our Posie Bags are arranged in hydrated floral foam. Keep the foam moist by slowly pouring a small amount of water into the center every 1–2 days. Display in a cool spot away from direct sunlight or heat. Don't remove the flowers from the foam — they'll last best when left as designed.
