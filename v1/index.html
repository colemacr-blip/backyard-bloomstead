More than flowers.

We grow cut flowers, create floral art, and build meaningful gifts — all from a real backyard garden. Every product in the Posie line is inspired by the memory of a small rescue cat who loved to nap among the zinnias and dahlias.

Meet Posie

In 2024, a small rescue cat appeared from under the cottage across the street. She endured an ice storm, slowly learned to trust us, and found her sanctuary among the zinnias and dahlias of our garden.

Though her time with us was brief, Posie's spirit lives on in every bloom we grow and every gift we create.
