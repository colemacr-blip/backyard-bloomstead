The Bloom Bar

Our Bloom Bar is an interactive flower-picking experience where you choose your own blooms and colored jar to build a personalized Petite Posie — a small, meaningful arrangement that's all your own.

Each jar comes filled with your selected stems, hydrated and ready to brighten any space. No two Posies are exactly alike.

The Bloom Bar is also a meaningful employment opportunity through Solin Sanctuary — connecting community members with purposeful work in a nurturing environment.

Flower Care

How long will my arrangement last?

5 to 7 days. Keep in cool indoor temperatures, change water daily, and trim stems every couple of days to help them drink.

How do I care for flowers in a Posie Bag?

Keep the foam moist — add a small amount of water daily to the foam base. Display away from direct sunlight. Do not remove flowers from the foam as this can shorten their life.
