Our Story

More than flowers.

What began with a few raised beds and a love of blooms grew into something deeper — a whole world of meaning tucked inside every petal. The Posie line was born from this garden: small, intentional arrangements that carry the language of flowers into everyday life.

Each design begins in the dirt. Real plants, real seasons, real imperfection. We press memories into print and wrap them in the vocabulary of floriography — the ancient art of communicating through flowers. Here, nothing is just decoration.

The Heart of the Brand

Meet Posie

She arrived from under a cottage, small and uncertain, and made our backyard garden her sanctuary. Posie — our beloved rescue cat — became the quiet muse behind the whole Bloomstead line, padding between raised beds and watching zinnias climb toward the sky.

Her story is one of resilience, joy, and the way a simple garden can become a place of profound belonging. The Posie collection is named in her honor, and every bloom we grow carries a trace of her spirit.

The Language of Flowers

Flower Meanings

Long before greeting cards, flowers carried messages. A sprig of rosemary meant remembrance. A yellow rose, friendship. A zinnia, lasting affection. We've always believed that flowers are a language unto themselves — and we want to help you speak it fluently.

Our flower meanings guide is a living reference — growing season by season with the garden itself. Look up the blooms in your bouquet and discover what they say about you.
